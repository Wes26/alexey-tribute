Alexey Pajitnov

Developer of Tetris

Alexey Pajitnov was the creator of Tetris. Tetris the most popular puzzle game of all time was created in 1984. Tetris was first available in the Soviet Union. Then the "God Father" of puzzel games appeared in the West in 1986. Due to not recieving alot of recognition, I made a tribute page for Alexey!
